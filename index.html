County Website Redesign

This is a responsive redesign of Loving County’s resources website. The original website had severe usability and accessibility issues so there was significant room for improvement. I developed the prototypes for the design with Balsamiq and Figma, and made the interface with HTML and CSS.

CVS Self-Checkout Contextual Inquiry

This is a contextual inquiry of self checkout machines as CVS. I observed users of the machines at my local CVS and asked them a series of questions following their checkout. From these I developed a few different personas and constructed a storyboard showing one persona’s user experience.

This is work I’ve done in designing a national environmental health map. Present maps have significant usability and data issues which I am hoping to address. In this project, I develop sketches for different designs and a plan for conducting user testing.
